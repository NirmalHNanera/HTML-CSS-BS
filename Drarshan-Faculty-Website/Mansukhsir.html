PROF. MANSUKH BARASARA

Professor

M.E.(Structural),BE(Civil)

Experience Working Since
34+ Years June-2009

Barasara sir, popularly known as ENCYCLOPEDIA OF DARSHAN UNIVERSITY, has an extensive and exhaustive knowledge not only in the engineering field but also in Veda, Ayurveda, Sanskrit, History, Mathematics, etc...

He has done B.E. in Civil Engineering and M.E. in Structural Engineering. He has worked with Ajanta Manufacturers (Morbi), Atlanta Limited (Mumbai) in the starting phase of his career. He has also worked as a lecturer in L. E. college - Morbi, GEC - Modasa, V. V. P. Engineering college - Rajkot for a span of 15 years.

Outstanding knowledge and wide experience of handling Civil Engineering projects, Structural Designing of various buildings and material testing, he is actively involved in consultancy & testing projects.
