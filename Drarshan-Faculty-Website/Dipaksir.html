PROFILE

PROF. DIPAK JIVANI

Head of The Department

Ph.D(Persuing),M.Tech (CASAD),B.E.(Civil)
Experience Working Since
13+ Years Jan-2013

Prof. Dipak Jivani obtained M.Tech. (Structure) in 2008 from Nirma University. He has been associated with the Darshan University-Rajkot since 2013 and presently working as Assistant Professor. He has 8 years of experience including Profession experience at TATA Consulting Engineers. He has professional experience in structural designing of various industrial structures, multi-storied residential buildings, machine foundations and water retaining structures. He has more than Seventeen publications to his credit at National and International conferences and journals. 11 Master degrees were awarded under his guidance. His areas of interest include Earthquake engineering, Structural Dynamics, Structure analysis and Design.

Area of Specialization Structural Analysis & Design, Earthquake Engineering

Subjects Taught

Mechanics of Solids, Design of Structures I & II, Earthquake Engineering,, Prestressed Concrete, Advanced Design of Concrete Structures, Structural Dynamics and Earthquake Engineering, Structural Software Lab

Current Activities

Teaching at Undergraduate and Postgraduate level, Research guidance to M.E students, Consultancy services offered by Department and administrative work
