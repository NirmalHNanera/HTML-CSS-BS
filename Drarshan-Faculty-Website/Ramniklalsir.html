PROFILE

DR.RAMNIKLAL DHAMSANIYA

PRESIDENT-DARSHAN UNIVERSITY

Ph.D(Structure,ME (Structure),B.E.(Civil)
Experience Working Since
36+ Years Jul-2009

Dhamsaniya sir is one of the popular personalities in the field of engineering education. He has started his career as a lecturer at L. E. College Morbi and worked for 10 years. In 1996, he moved to V. V. P. engineering college where he has played vital role in the infrastructural & academic development of the institute. He has also worked with Atmiya college for 2 years.

In the year 2009, with the intention of giving a platform for committed education, he has started the Darshan Institute of Engineering & Technology with a team of dedicated faculty.

He has played a key role in the development of structural consultancy, environmental auditing, energy auditing, material testing, and highway consultancy, etc with various Government, Semi-Government and reputed private organizations in order to connect the academics & industries.
